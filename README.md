# NOKUT_Dashboard_Tableau
#Every autumn, NOKUT conducts a national student survey (Studiebarometeret) on study
quality on behalf of the Ministry of Education and Research. The survey covers almost all
Norwegian universities and colleges and ask students to evaluate various quality dimensions
such as teaching, supervision, learning environment, commitment, motivation and so on. 
# The dashboards are meant to help Management assess how Kristiania is doing compared to other universities
and colleges on the various quality dimensions and benchmark their best performing and
worst performing study programs.
